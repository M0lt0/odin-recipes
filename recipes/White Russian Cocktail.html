White Russian Cocktail

description

Wondering what's in a white Russian? The Big Lebowski was onto something with this creamy coffee-flavored cocktail. A healthy dose of vodka in the mix assures that 'the dude abides.'

Per Serving: 338 calories; protein 0.6g; carbohydrates 12g; fat 11.1g; cholesterol 40.8mg; sodium 22.1mg. Full Nutrition

Ingredients
  • 1 cup ice
  • 2 fluid ounces vodka
  • 1 fluid ounce coffee-flavored liqueur
  • 1 fluid ounce heavy cream
Steps
  1. Fill an old-fashioned glass with ice. Pour in vodka and coffee liqueur. Top off with cream.
